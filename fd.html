Face Detection

There are three forms of face recognition There are face detection, face extraction, and face recognition. Face extraction entails capturing features from the camera. Face detection entails deleting the background and concentrating on the foreground, leaving only the face region. Finding faces is the most crucial component of face detection. Faces can be discovered using a variety of techniques. Despite the fact that face recognition is the most important phase of image processing, the methods employed to carry it out need to be updated in order to increase its performance and reduce the obstacles it faces. Face detection algorithms rely significantly on face detection accuracy. This system I used python and Opencv.
